Cristiano Ronaldo scores another hat-trick for Portugal as Mario Rui heralds him as 'best in world'.

Ronaldo scored three goals as Portugal hammered hapless Lithuania.

Cristiano Ronaldo is the best footballer in the world, his Portugal team-mate Mario Rui has claimed.

It was Ronaldo's 55th career hat-trick and his ninth for the Selecao - and after the match Napoli full-back Rui insisted the 34-year-old remains the best in the world.

"He is the best in the world, never tired of winning, wanting more, and it is this hunger that makes him what he is," the Napoli left-back said, via A Bola. "We are happy to have him on the team and I hope he will continue to give us joy as he has done so far."
