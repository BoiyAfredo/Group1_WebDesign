Demon Slayer: Kimetsu No Yaiba

is a Japanese manga series by Koyoharu Gotōge. This manga began to be published in Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine published by Shueisha since February 2016, and has been bundled into seventeen tankōbon volumes as of October 2019. An anime adaptation produced by Ufotable was aired from April 6 to September 29, 2019. The sequel to the big screen was announced when the last episode was broadcast.
